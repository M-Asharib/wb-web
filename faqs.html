Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our services, process, and pricing

What services do you offer?

We specialize in custom digital solutions including web design, mobile app development (iOS/Android), cross-platform applications, and desktop software development for Windows, macOS, and Linux. Our services also include UI/UX design, branding, and ongoing maintenance/support.

How do I get started with a project?

Starting a project is simple: contact us through our website form or schedule a free consultation. We'll discuss your requirements, goals, and timeline. After understanding your needs, we'll provide a detailed proposal with project scope, timeline, and pricing.

How long does a website design project take?

The timeline varies based on project complexity. A basic brochure website typically takes 4-6 weeks, while e-commerce sites or complex web applications may take 8-12 weeks or more. During our initial consultation, we'll provide a more accurate timeline based on your specific requirements.

Do you provide website maintenance after launch?

Yes, we offer ongoing maintenance packages that include security updates, content updates, performance optimization, and technical support. We can customize a maintenance plan based on your needs, from basic security monitoring to full-service management.

Should I build a native or cross-platform mobile app?

Native apps (Swift for iOS, Kotlin for Android) offer the best performance and access to device features but require separate codebases. Cross-platform (Flutter/React Native) is more cost-effective for simpler apps and faster development. We'll recommend the best approach based on your budget, timeline, and feature requirements.

How do you handle app store submissions?

We handle the entire submission process to the Apple App Store and Google Play Store, including preparing all required assets, creating developer accounts (if needed), and ensuring your app meets all guidelines. We'll guide you through any necessary steps on your end and manage the submission until approval.

What technologies do you use for desktop applications?

For Windows: .NET Framework, WPF, UWP. For macOS: Swift, Objective-C. For cross-platform: Electron, Qt, Java. We choose the technology stack based on your performance requirements, target platforms, and integration needs with existing systems.

Can you create applications that work offline?

Absolutely. Desktop applications are ideal for offline functionality. We can design systems with local data storage that sync with cloud services when internet connectivity is available, or create completely standalone applications depending on your requirements.

How much does a custom website or application cost?

Project costs vary significantly based on scope and complexity. Basic websites start around $3,000, while complex web applications can range $15,000-$50,000+. Mobile apps typically start at $10,000, and desktop applications at $15,000. We provide transparent quotes after understanding your specific requirements.

Do you offer payment plans or financing?

Yes, we offer flexible payment options. Most projects are divided into milestones with payments due at each stage. We can also discuss extended payment plans for larger projects. All payment terms will be clearly outlined in our proposal before work begins.
